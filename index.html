The Process

Simple as Four Steps

01

Reach Out & Tell Us

WhatsApp or email your requirement — a tailor design, a social media find, a product to source, or a family pickup.

02

We Shop or Stitch

Tailoring orders go to Sruthi's studio; shopping orders are sourced from India's best online and offline stores.

03

Store & Consolidate

Items arrive at your Desi To Videsi Hyderabad locker. We inspect, photograph and hold everything until you're ready.

04

Packed & Delivered

We bundle into one optimised package and courier worldwide — tracked door-to-door in 3–7 business days.

Why This Works

Sending multiple shipments from India is expensive and complicated. We act as your permanent India address — receiving, holding and bundling into a single cost-optimised consignment.
